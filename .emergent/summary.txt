<analysis>**original_problem_statement:** 
My app should be able to keep track of what medicine I have in stock, as well as be able to create a weekly schedule where you can tell which preparations you have taken, and when. The user must have the option to tell what time of day they are taking medicine (e.g. Morning, Noon, Evening and Night), and then tell which medicine is taken, and at what time. The user should be able to choose how long before they run out of a preparation they should get a reminder to order medicine. There must also be symbols next to each preparation that show the status of when they expire. The user must choose a pin code on first use, and possibly the option of biometric login thereafter. After choosing a pin code, the program must have your name and email entered. The email is used to create a new pin code.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- Standalone app with own database.
- Use SendGrid/Resend for email.
- Dark theme.
- Support Danish and English languages.
- Ability to edit dose on the schedule.
- Show total mg on the schedule form.
- Support different doses on different days of the week.
- Generate a printable, landscape, weekly dosage schedule.
- The system should automatically deduct medicine stock (no Mark as taken button).
- The print schedule should be generated as a downloadable PDF file.
- The dashboard view should not have a month/year calendar header, only day names.
- Medicine forms should support start dates, end dates, and recurrence patterns.
- The schedule should support special, recurring ordinations based on dates rather than fixed weekdays.

**User's preferred language**: Danish
